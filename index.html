Our mission

The critical state of our planet is alarming. The imminent disappearance of fossil fuels will disrupt our infrastructures. The migration of peoples affected by climate change will intensify geopolitical conflicts. Essential resources such as water, food and medicines will become scarce. Humanity's survival is at stake, requiring individual preparation to protect our families.

Build your survival library

Survival is accessible to all, thanks to preparation, the acquisition of knowledge and the right equipment. In a future where access to knowledge will be limited, it is essential to create a personal library of essential books to preserve ancestral knowledge and survive. We call these libraries foundations.

"Anticipating collapse" collection

This collection is designed to impart the knowledge essential for survival. These books should not be kept "just in case", but read and assimilated now. Proper preparation can often be the deciding factor between life and death. Make sure you're among those who are fully prepared.
